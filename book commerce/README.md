### About

This Book Commerce project is designed to facilitate the buying and selling of books among students, whether you're a senior looking to sell your old textbooks or a junior seeking to buy used books at a reduced cost. The platform is user-friendly, allowing both buyers and sellers to easily manage their transactions.

**Purpose of this Website:**
- Enable students to buy and sell books online.
- Maintain a history of book transactions.
- Simplify the management of book inventory.
- Provide an easy-to-use interface.
- Demonstrate the use of Http Servlets in Java.
- Developed as a mini-project using Java, JDBC, and Servlets.

**Admin Features:**
- Add new books to the inventory.
- View the list of available books.
- Remove books from the inventory.
- Adjust the quantity of books.

**User Features:**
- Register a new account.
- Log in to the platform.
- Browse available books.
- Select books to purchase.
- Choose the quantity of books.
- Complete the purchase.
- Receive a payment receipt. 
<br>
Whether you are a senior looking to pass on your books to the next generation or a junior searching for affordable textbooks, this platform is designed to meet your needs.
